Club Info Summary

Mission Statement Our goal is to improve at math, participate in competitions, and have fun. Our math team participates regularly in mathamathics competitions where we strive to do better each time. This club is for students who are passionate and want to expand their knowledge about math. In out club students can learn advanced topics that they wouldn't have in their regular classes.
